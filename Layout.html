Fixed Gear Forever

16 Abril, 2011

The veloheld combines minimalist desing with superb quality. Devoid of excesive graphics and gear shift components, the veloheld product range delights us with his beauty and simplicity. The black and white (Yin and Yang?) bicycle features a short wheelbase, a single gear and a narrow handlebar... All you need to explore the city streets.

For those who want to create their bike themselves, the veloheld frames course in three sizes and two colors and are the perfect starting point.
