Briards

By Ivy Duckett

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardiand of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries sto herd and to protect sheep. the breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers becouse of its ine sense of hearing, Briards wre used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson, all owned briards.
